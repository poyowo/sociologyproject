"Why did you pick this sociologist?"

Arnado stood out to me because her study was in many ways a parallel to the experiences my parents had gone through when immigrating to the United States. They recieved overall better opportunities in the west, and while they ultimately had to sacrifice seeing their families for lengthy gaps of time, they decided that the tradeoff was worth it.
